    US Corn Yields

    Summary Because I grew up on a farm and have always been interested in agriculture, I wanted to take a look at corn yields and how they were effected by variables in data that I could find. Luckily the USDA has a relatively extensive dataset on crop production and yields by state and also includes information such as if the crops were irrigated or not. They have a query tool which I used to get the raw data which is also available in this GitHub repo.
